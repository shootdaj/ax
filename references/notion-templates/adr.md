# Architecture Decision Records

## What are ADRs?

Architecture Decision Records capture significant architectural decisions made during development. Each record documents the context, decision, and consequences.

## Template

### ADR-NNN: _Title_

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-NNN

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

**Context:**
_What is the issue or question being addressed?_

**Decision:**
_What was decided?_

**Consequences:**
_What are the positive and negative outcomes of this decision?_

**Alternatives considered:**
_What other options were evaluated and why were they rejected?_
